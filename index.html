About Us

We are an investment company focused on consolidating the fragmented assisted living industry.

Many assisted living facilities are owned by independent mom & pop operators — we unify them under one professionally managed company. Our focus is on making a series of strategic acquisitions and executing rollups, starting in Pennsylvania and expanding into neighboring states.

Our Strategy

01

Identify

Target profitable, well-run assisted living businesses owned by independent operators looking for a transition.

02

Acquire

Execute acquisitions with a focus on fair valuations, smooth transitions, and preserving quality of care.

03

Unify

Bring acquired businesses under our umbrella, implementing standardized operations while maintaining personalized care.

04

Scale

Expand geographically from Pennsylvania into neighboring states, replicating the proven model across new markets.
